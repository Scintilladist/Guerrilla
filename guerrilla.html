About Us

Our multidisciplinary team has expertise in business, engineering, data science, user experience, product design, and sculpture. The Guerrilla device is easy to install and maintain, and we're excited to work with partners to bring it to more cities.

Impact

Based on predictive analysis, installing Guerrilla devices in 250 roadside drains in London can reduce the turbidity of the Thames river by 37%. That's a significant impact on water quality, and we're committed to expanding our reach to make even more of a difference. Our partners have seen impressive results with our devices, and we're confident that we can help protect waterways around the world.
